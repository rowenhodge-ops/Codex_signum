# ORG’s AI & Automation Strategy
#Org 
#CDN 

At ORG, our strategy is clear: we help organisations become smarter, more resilient, and more efficient by combining automation, AI, and data with a people-first approach.

We believe technology alone doesn’t deliver transformation. True impact comes when AI and automation are applied to solve real business problems — improving outcomes for customers, employees, and the wider organisation. Every engagement starts with one key question: What problem are we solving? From there, we work backwards to select the right technology and design the right solution.

Our strategy rests on four pillars:

 

1. Problem-First, Not Tech-First

Too often, businesses start with a platform and then search for a use case. We flip this around. We begin with the challenge: whether it’s improving patient flow in healthcare, reducing risk in financial services, streamlining student services in education, or cutting delays in insurance claims processing. Once the problem is clear, we bring in the right mix of automation, AI, and human expertise to solve it.

 

2. Idea2Innovate Framework

Our structured innovation framework gives frontline staff and leaders a safe environment to turn “what if” ideas into fully costed business cases. It ensures that transformation strategies don’t just stay on paper — they become projects that deliver measurable impact.

-	Engages staff at every level to surface ideas.

-	Prioritises high-value opportunities using a transparent scoring model.

-	Builds delivery roadmaps with cost/benefit analysis.

-	Accelerates execution by linking ideas to the right technology partners.

 

3. Agentic AI + Automation with Trusted Partners

We’ve built an ecosystem of partners not by chance, but by design. Each was chosen because they bring something unique that complements our expertise and helps us solve problems for clients in a way that is scalable and sustainable.

-	UiPath – Our automation backbone

ORG is a UiPath Platinum Partner, the highest partner level. We chose UiPath because it offers the most complete automation platform in the market, combining RPA, document understanding, test automation, process mining, and AI agents in one enterprise-grade solution. It gives our clients the governance, compliance, and scalability they need to automate with confidence. UiPath is also trusted globally across healthcare, finance, government, and education — making it the right foundation for clients that need security and resilience built in.

-	Druid – Redefining human engagement

Druid sees ORG as one of their strategic global partners, which reflects how closely we work together. We selected Druid because conversational AI has become a critical part of digital transformation. People — whether they are patients, students, or customers — don’t want to wait on calls or fill in forms. They want immediate, accurate answers from AI assistants that are available 24/7. Druid allows us to bring that capability into industries like higher education, healthcare, and financial services, while integrating seamlessly into enterprise systems.

-	Deep Opinion (now Otera) – AI-first process intelligence

Otera is a core AI partner for ORG because of their ability to bring intelligence into high-volume, complex processes. We’ve worked with them on areas such as accounts payable and receivable, but also more specialised use cases like insurance claims processing. These processes are expensive, time-consuming, and prone to error — Otera’s AI agents cut through that complexity, reducing turnaround times, improving compliance, and freeing up people to focus on higher-value work.

Alongside these partnerships, we’ve built one of the strongest automation delivery teams in the market, with some of the best developers, solution architects, and BAs. Together, this mix of talent and trusted partnerships means we can deliver practical, business-led solutions that make a measurable difference.

 

4. Sustainable Change

We don’t just build solutions — we embed change. That means:

-	Training & capability building so clients can scale automation and AI in-house.

-	Governance & frameworks to ensure adoption is ethical, secure, and compliant.

-	Support & continuous improvement models so ROI grows over time.

 

In Summary

ORG’s AI & Automation strategy is about more than technology. It’s about:

-	Starting with business problems.

-	Turning ideas into action through Idea2Innovate.

-	Building solutions with a trusted ecosystem of partners — UiPath, Druid, and Otera — supported by one of the best delivery teams in the market.

-	Embedding sustainable change so organisations can scale with confidence.

This approach means our clients don’t just save money — they grow, they innovate, and they empower their people with AI.
