# Marketing Agent - Specification (PARKED)

**Purpose**: Content marketing automation (blog posts, case studies, LinkedIn content) and SEO optimization

**Build Trigger**: NOT YET - Awaiting product offering or inbound lead volume >20/month

---

## Overview

**PARKED** - Marketing Agent would automate:
1. **Blog post generation** from engagement notes → thought leadership content
2. **Case study creation** (anonymized client success stories)
3. **LinkedIn content** (weekly posts, comment engagement)
4. **SEO optimization** (keyword research, on-page SEO recommendations)

**Why parked**: Current business model is relationship-driven (warm intros), not inbound marketing

**Decision Gates**: Product offering (courses, templates) OR high inbound volume

---

## Anticipated Capabilities

### Tool 1: Blog Post Generator
- Converts engagement learnings → SEO-optimized blog posts
- Generates 2-3 posts/week automatically

### Tool 2: Case Study Writer
- Anonymizes client engagements → compelling success stories
- Includes metrics, testimonials, ROI data

### Tool 3: LinkedIn Content Scheduler
- Weekly thought leadership posts (AI governance, higher ed trends)
- Engages with comments (sentiment-aware responses)

### Tool 4: SEO Optimizer
- Keyword research (Google Trends, search volume)
- On-page SEO recommendations (meta descriptions, headers, internal linking)

---

## Why Parked?

- **Relationship-driven business** (warm intros > inbound leads)
- **No product to market** (consulting services, not courses/templates)
- **Opportunity cost** (building Marketing Agent delays core agent deployment)

**Re-evaluation**: When launching product offering (courses, templates) OR inbound volume >20/month
