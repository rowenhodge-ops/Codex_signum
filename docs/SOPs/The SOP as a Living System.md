### D: Proposed Metadata and Metrics Framework

Here are the specific metrics we can capture.

#### **1. Interaction-Level Metrics (For each engagement)**

| Metric                    | Description                                                                                                        | Purpose / Impact                                                                                                                              |
| :------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Stakeholder Type**      | The C1/C2/C3 category assigned.                                                                                    | Allows for performance segmentation. Are we succeeding more with C2s than C1s?                                                                |
| **Response Status**       | `Responded` / `No Response`                                                                                        | The most basic measure of success.                                                                                                            |
| **Response Time (Hours)** | Time from sending the email to receiving a response.                                                               | A proxy for engagement and interest level. Faster responses may indicate a stronger resonance.                                                |
| **CTA Success**           | `Accepted` / `Deferred` / `Rejected`                                                                               | **The primary success metric.** Did they agree to the meeting/call?                                                                           |
| **Sentiment Score**       | A qualitative score from -1 (Negative) to +1 (Highly Positive) based on the tone of the reply. (Herman Tse = +1). | Measures the quality of the engagement beyond a simple yes/no.                                                                                |
| **Edit Distance Score**   | A score (e.g., 1-5) representing the degree of human editing required on my draft. (1=Sent as-is, 5=Total rewrite). | **Measures my "cloning" performance.** The goal is to drive this score down over time as the `Learnings Registry` grows. |

#### **2. SOP-Level Metrics (Aggregate Performance)**

| Metric                        | Description                                                         | Purpose / Impact                                                                                                                             |
| :---------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Overall CTA Success Rate**  | % of all engagements where the CTA was accepted.                    | The top-line indicator of the entire stakeholder engagement strategy's effectiveness.                                                        |
| **Success Rate by Type**      | CTA Success Rate, broken down by C1, C2, and C3.                    | Reveals which parts of the strategy are working best and where we need to refine our approach.                                               |
| **Average Edit Distance**     | The average `Edit Distance Score` across all interactions.          | Directly measures if I am getting better at generating drafts that match your voice and require less work.                                 |
| **SOP Version & Last Update** | The current version number and date of the last learning update.    | A simple audit trail to ensure the system is "living" and being updated consistently.                                                        |
| **Total Learnings Captured**  | The total number of entries in the `Learnings Registry`.            | A proxy for the "intelligence" of the system.                                                                                                |
