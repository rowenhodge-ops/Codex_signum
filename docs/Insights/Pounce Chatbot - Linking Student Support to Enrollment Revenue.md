Summary of the Connection

1. **Soft ROI Focus (Student Support):** GSU faced "summer melt," where admitted students failed to enroll due to navigating complex administrative deadlines for financial aid, housing, and registration. The solution, **"Pounce,"** provided **proactive, personalized support** via text message and answered a wide range of questions **24/7**. This enhanced support service is the primary Soft ROI delivered.

2. **Hard ROI Realization (Enrollment Uplift):** By effectively eliminating administrative friction and ensuring students completed necessary pre-enrollment steps, the chatbot directly resulted in **3.3% more admitted students choosing to enroll**. Since university revenue is heavily dependent on student fees and charges, an increase in enrollment and re-enrollment directly translates into a significant, measurable financial benefit (Hard ROI). The GSU case study proves that a Soft ROI project can deliver the **strongest Hard ROI outcome**.

3. **Persistence Value:** The study also tracked the continuing value, finding that enrolled students who used Pounce were **3% more likely to persist (re-enroll)**, further contributing to long-term institutional revenue and stability.

This case study demonstrates the necessity of using a **Hybrid ROI Model** that prioritizes mission-centric outcomes, recognizing that non-financial benefits are powerful **leading indicators** of future financial performance.
