| **Pounce Chatbot** | **Georgia State University (GSU)** | GSU, a large public university, faced the challenge of "summer melt"—admitted students failing to enroll due to administrative hurdles. The project was an **AI-enhanced chatbot** designed to **proactively engage students** via text message, sending personalized, timely reminders about critical deadlines (financial aid, registration, housing) and answering questions 24/7. The goal was to provide scalable, personalized support to incoming students. | Incoming students who had access to the chatbot were **3.3% more likely to enroll** in the fall semester compared to a control group. Furthermore, continuing students who used the tool were **3% more likely to re-enroll**. | ,,        |

This case study is highly impactful because it demonstrated a clear, quantifiable return on investment (ROI) by directly addressing a critical enrollment and retention problem ("summer melt"). The outcome proves that a "Soft ROI" project, focused on student support and experience, can deliver the strongest "Hard ROI" outcome through improved enrollment uplift and persistence.
