Here is a comprehensive research plan focused on operational readiness.

This list is divided into the four key areas of knowledge you need to master to run the business: Regulatory & Compliance, The "Consultancy OS" Stack, Partner & Implementation Governance, and Core Business Operations.

### 1. Regulatory, Data & AI Compliance (Australia)

This research validates the "rules of the road" for handling client data and providing AI advice in Australia.

- Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
    

- Deep Dive: Analysis of the 13 APPs under the Privacy Act 1988.
    
- Sub-topics: How do these principles specifically apply to handling client data (e.g., university student PII) for the purpose of analysis and model training? What are the legal requirements for data collection (APP 3), use/disclosure (APP 6), and security (APP 11)?
    

- AI Regulatory Landscape (Current State)
    

- Deep Dive: Analysis of the Australian government's current stance on AI regulation.
    
- Sub-topics: Review the "Safe and Responsible AI in Australia" discussion paper and the government's response. What are the current advisory guidelines for responsible AI (e.g., accountability, transparency, fairness)? This informs the "strategic advice" portion of your $45k Roadmap.
    

- Data Sovereignty & Security Standards
    

- Deep Dive: Determine the specific data residency and security requirements for your target clients (HEI, government, finance).
    
- Sub-topics: Can client data leave Australia? (This directly impacts your choice of cloud provider and SaaS tools). What are the contractual requirements for partners handling this data? Review the Australian Cyber Security Centre's (ACSC) "Essential Eight" as a baseline for security advice.
    

- Consumer Data Right (CDR) & Sectoral Rules
    

- Deep Dive: Assess the impact of specific data-sharing legislation.
    
- Sub-topics: While not immediately applicable to HEI operations, understanding the CDR framework demonstrates strategic foresight for future clients in banking. Research any specific data-handling rules for the Higher Education Support Act 2003 (HESA).
    

### 2. The "Consultancy OS" Stack (Technical Knowledge)

This research validates which tools you will select for your "Methodology + SaaS" IP, forming your technical point of view.

- Enterprise AI Platforms (Comparative Analysis)
    

- Deep Dive: A cost/benefit/sovereignty analysis of the "Big 3" enterprise AI platforms available in Australia.
    
- Sub-topics: Google Cloud Vertex AI vs. Microsoft Azure AI Studio vs. AWS Bedrock.
    
- Criteria: Australian region availability/latency, data sovereignty controls, model access (Gemini, GPT-4o, Claude 3), enterprise security, and pricing models (token vs. provisioned).
    

- Orchestration & Agentic Frameworks
    

- Deep Dive: Solution architecture comparison for building reliable, repeatable "Playbooks."
    
- Sub-topics: LangChain (pros/cons of its abstraction) vs. LlamaIndex (data-centric) vs. Python-native SDKs. Research emerging agentic orchestration tools (e.g., LangGraph, CrewAI) and assess their production readiness for $150k enterprise pilots.
    

- LLM Routers & Function Calling
    

- Deep Dive: Best-practice patterns for "agentic" workflows that interact with client systems.
    
- Sub-topics: Research the reliability of semantic routing (classifying intent) vs. model-native function calling. This is critical for scoping the integration component of your pilots.
    

- 'LLMOps' & Observability (Minimum Viable Stack)
    

- Deep Dive: Determine the "must-have" metrics and tools to govern a pilot (as required by your "Success Guarantee").
    
- Criteria: What are the non-negotiable metrics? (e.g., cost per-call, latency, token use, hallucination/drift detection, PII leakage).
    
- Tooling: Comparative analysis of platform-native tools (e.g., Azure Monitor) vs. third-party specialists (e.g., LangSmith, Arize AI) to recommend to your partners.
    

### 3. Implementation & Partner Governance

This research builds the "Playbook" for how you manage your "Implementation Partnership" and govern delivery.

- Partner Vetting & "Certification" Rubric
    

- Deep Dive: Create the artifacts to vet and onboard your "Primary" and "Bench" partners.
    
- Sub-topics: What is the ideal partner profile? (e.g., must be platform-certified on [your chosen stack], must provide a named "Delivery Lead," must have prior public-sector/HEI experience). What does your "Delivery Lead Certification" workshop actually teach?
    

- AI SOW & Governance Templates
    

- Deep Dive: Create the master legal and project documents.
    
- Sub-topics: How to draft the $150k SOW with a 3-Tier pricing model. How to contractually define the "Client Success Guarantee" and its KPIs. What are the non-negotiable weekly reporting artifacts the "Delivery Lead" must provide you?
    

- IP & Contractual Frameworks
    

- Deep Dive: Research and draft the non-negotiable legal clauses for all partner and client contracts.
    
- Sub-topics: Template "IP Assignment" (Work-for-Hire) and "Knowledge Retention" (code handover) clauses for partners. How to legally define your IP (the "Consultancy OS" methodology) as separate from the client's IP (the solution and their data).
    

### 4. Core Business Operations (Back Office)

This research validates the cost and setup requirements for running the practice itself.

- Insurance Quantification (Critical Risk)
    

- Deep Dive: Get actual quotes for your specific business model.
    
- Sub-topics: Solicit quotes for a $5M+ coverage policy bundle including:
    

1. Professional Indemnity (PI): Covers your strategic advice.
    
2. Cyber Liability: Covers a data breach.
    
3. Errors & Omissions (E&O): Covers the failure of your implemented solution (critical for the "Success Guarantee").
    

- Key Question: What are the specific exclusions related to "AI advice" or "hallucinations"?
    

- Corporate & Tax Structure
    

- Deep Dive: Optimal corporate structure for a solo principal + contractor model in Australia.
    
- Sub-topics: Pty Ltd (for liability protection). Research GST registration, PAYG withholding (for contractors vs. retainers), and state-by-state Payroll Tax implications as your partner retainers and "Flex" payments scale.
    

- "Practice Ops" Tech Stack
    

- Deep Dive: Select the minimum viable tools to run the business.
    
- Sub-topics: Accounting (Xero vs. Hnry), Project Governance (Asana, Trello, etc.), Secure Client Repository (M365 SharePoint vs. Google Workspace), and CRM (a simple pipeline tracker).
