## Unacceptable Structures (Walk Away)

❌ **Day Rate Masquerading as Fixed Price**

- Example: I charge client $15,000 Initium, ORG takes $5,000 cut, Christian takes $2,000, I receive $8,000
- This recreates day-rate economics ($8k for 2 weeks = $800/day effective) with zero control
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **Client Relationship Transfer**

- Example: I introduce ORG to Herman/Monash, ORG subsequently claims ownership and contracts directly
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **Mandatory Delivery Partner Lock-In**

- Example: Contract requires I use ORG for all Fabrica delivery with no alternative partner option
- Destroys my tool-agnostic positioning and creates vendor dependency
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **IP Licensing or Methodology Sharing**

- Example: ORG requests access to Codex structure, Initium templates, or Colophon process to "standardize delivery"
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **Pipeline Building for Christian's Job Security**

- Example: Informal expectation that I generate sales leads to help Christian secure permanent ORG role
- I'm not a volunteer sales development resource
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **Double-Cut Economics**

- Example: ORG takes 30% introduction fee, Christian separately invoices 10% finder's fee
- **Walk away immediately**

❌ **Forum Access as Payment**

- Example: "Exposure" at COO/CFO forum positioned as compensation instead of commercial opportunity
- Exposure doesn't pay bills; if forum creates leads, refer to Revenue Split rules
- **Walk away if this is primary "value" offered**

**Red Flag Answer**: "We've had good conversations but haven't put formal proposals together yet"  
**Green Flag Answer**: "They're interested in AI readiness work but concerned about open-ended consulting fees"

**Question 4**: "If I deliver a diagnostic to one of ORG's pipeline universities, who owns that client relationship going forward?"

**Nuclear Question**: His answer reveals everything about partnership vs. exploitation intent.

**Red Flag Answer**: "Well, since it's through ORG's existing relationship, they would own the account"

**Question 5**: "What margin does ORG typically take on partner-delivered work?"
