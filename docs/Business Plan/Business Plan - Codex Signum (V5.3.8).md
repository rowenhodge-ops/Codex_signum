# Business Plan - Codex Signum (V5.3.8)
**

## Compounding Value Model

### 1.0 Executive Summary

**Business Identity**: Codex Signum.
**Positioning**: A systems-driven, strategic architect 
**Value Proposition**: We solve the COO/CFO's key challenges and opportunities by de-risking current transformation ROI's; identifying and implementing E2E transformation; all with a fixed cost guaranteeing  outcomes.

The business is primarily two pillars:

1. The "Strategic Advisory" (P1): A Trusted Advisor with a focus to move from CapEx to Opex with clients
2. The data-native evolving engine (P2): An automated IP factory that ingests data from the to evolve the Codex.  The key focus on capturing contextual data that is unique and leveraging it.

Y1 Target (from V5.2): c. $570k Revenue / c. $296k Profit (52% EBITDA).

### 2.0 Design Principles

- Focus on Value: With the 'billionaire unicorn' mentality relentlessly focus on automating and out sourcing the low-value activities and prioritizing profit.
- Build the business like a Neural net: Weighted nodes and connectors; feedback loop; bullheads; graceful degradation; 
- All nodes will be connected: all activities are nodes in the Codex. Data for all elements must be captured and poured into the feedback loop.
- Privacy: Privacy is paramount. Any data captured and utilises in the Codex must be anonymised.
- Continually work at automating all processes.
- Proprietary Data: The Codex's value is contingent on capturing proprietary, high-context, non-public data.
- The Business Tax: The Advisory tactical activities are the "tax".  It generates the revenue and data to fund the engine build, which is the "reward." [If feel like this is a risk mitigant rather than a design principle]
- Fixed-Price Model: Only sell fixed-price, "value-based" products. This forces high-margin delivery, necessitates "Orchestration," and aligns us with the C-suite EBITDA mindset.
- Fiduciary Accountability (The "Colophon"): We are not vendors; we are fiduciaries. Our engagements are governed by a non-negotiable Colophon sign-off, which validates client ROI and captures the high-weight data for the Codex. This is a non-negotiable act of a "Trusted Advisor."

### 3.0 The Advisory: Product Offering



- Product 1: AI Strategy Navigator (The "Hook")
	- Price: Free.
- Function: V1.0 of the "Hidden Business" (Codex). A "smart survey" that ingests a lead's "messy problem" (data acquisition).
- Goal: To convert a cold lead into a warm C3 call.
- Product 2: $15k Initium (The "Wedge" / Diagnostic)
- Price: $15,000 (Fixed Price, 100% upfront).
- Function: A 2-week "Stale Data" & Forecasting Architecture Audit.
- Deiverable: A "Business Architect" map of the "messy problem" and 3-5 solution hypotheses (the SOW for Fabrica).
- Goal: 1) Solve the client's problem. 2) Acquire deep Codex data. 3) Upsell to Fabrica.
- Product 3: $150k Fabrica (The "Project" / Implementation)
- Price: $100k - $200k (Fixed Price).
- Function: A 90-day "Trojan Horse" implementation of a single high-ROI solution from the Initium.
- Goal: 1) Deliver a massive, validated ROI. 2) Secure the Colophon. 3) Flip to Vigilia.
- Product 4: $200k+ Vigilia (The "Retainer" / OpEx)
- Price: $10k - $30k/month ($120k - $360k/year).
- Function: "Governance-as-a-Service." We monitor, manage, and optimize the Fabrica solution.
- Goal: Secure long-term, high-margin, predictable recurring revenue.

### 4.0 The "Hidden Business": IP Architecture (V5.3)

This is the "Back Stage" (Praxis Forge) leverage.

- Asset 1: The Codex (The "Moat")
- Tech: Firestore + Vertex AI (RAG).
- Function: The central "knowledge graph" of all proprietary client data.
- Fuel: Ingests "low-weight" data from the Navigator and "high-weight" data from the Colophon.
- Asset 2: The Colophon (The "Validation Engine")
- Tech: V1.0 Google Form, V2.0 Node.js app.
- Function: A non-negotiable governance step (per P5) that validates client-signed-off ROI.
- Goal: Converts "project data" into "high-weight, Codex-ready, proprietary IP."
- Asset 3: The Codex Index (The "Automated Marketing")
- Tech: A secure Cloud Function bridge from the Codex to the public website.
- Function: Anonymizes and displays validated, Colophon-signed-off ROI metrics on the public website.
- Goal: Automates credibility and marketing (fulfills "I want people to come to me").
### 5.0 Critical Risks & "Red Team" Mitigation

1. Risk: The "Technician's Fatal Assumption" (Mentor's Trap).
- Description: "Working in the business" (delivering Initiums) instead of "working on the business" (building the Codex).
    
- Mitigation: P3. The P1-Funds-P2 Protocol. A non-negotiable "P2 - Night Job" time-block to "Orchestrate" (per E-Myth) and build Praxis Forge.
    

2. Risk: IP Leakage / "Mentor" Problem.
    

- Description: Mentor/competitors stealing "Back Stage" IP.
    
- Mitigation: P2. The "Front Stage / Back Stage" Protocol. We never discuss Praxis Forge or the Codex externally. We "grey rock" all IP questions.
    

3. Risk: GTM "Cold Start" Failure.
    

- Description: Failure to land the first $15k Initium due to "peddling" to C4 buyers.
    
- Mitigation: SOP - Gravity Model. We only "cold-call the moons" (C1/C3) via "intel-bartering," not "peddling."
    

4. Risk: HEI Concentration Risk.
    

- Description: The "HEI Whale" (UoW) fails, stalling the 90-day plan.
    
- Mitigation: GTM Strategy V1.0. We run parallel P1 discovery against "Logistics & Industrial" and "Healthcare Admin" to ensure a robust pipeline.
