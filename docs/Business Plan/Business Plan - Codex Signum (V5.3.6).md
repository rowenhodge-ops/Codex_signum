## 1.0 Executive Summary

Business Identity: Codex Signum Positioning: A "Holistic Strategist" (Value Prop) acting as a "Business Architect" (Function). We are a systems-driven consultancy that de-risks and delivers high-ROI operational transformation for the mid-market.

Business Model: A dual-track model where two businesses run in parallel:

1. The "Visible Business" (P1 - Priority 1): The "Front Stage" consultancy. This is the primary revenue and data-acquisition engine. It uses a fixed-price product ladder (Initium -> Consilium) to solve client problems.
    
2. The "Hidden Business" (P2 - Priority 2): The "Back Stage" IP Moat. This is the engine for automation (Praxis Forge). This system captures, validates (Colophon), and leverages proprietary data from P1 to create a compounding IP asset - the Codex Signum.
    

Strategic Goal (90 Days): To solve the "Cold Start" problem by securing the first "Beta Client" for a paid diagnostic (Initium), focusing on the Primary Target Segment (see gtm-strategy-v1.md).

This will provide the first high-value, proprietary dataset needed to fuel the "Hidden Business."

Secondary will be decoding the Principals previous projects e.g. LSS black belt home loans, and incorporating them into the Codex.

## 2.0 Design Principles

This plan is guided by four design principles.

- P1. The Proprietary Data Mandate (The "Moat"): The Codex's strategic value is contingent on high-quality, contextual data that cannot be found on the internet.
- 
- Its value is derived from proprietary Initium (diagnostic) and Fabrica (implementation) data. The Colophon is the primary governance node for validating this data's quality and ROI. Publicly-scraped data is for low-level context only.
- Data from 

    
- P2. The "Front Stage / Back Stage" Protocol (The "Black Box"): The "Hidden Business" (Praxis Forge, Codex, tech stacks, this plan) is classified. It is never discussed externally. It is the "how," not the "what." All external conversations (mentors, partners, clients) are only about the "Front Stage" (Initium product, "holistic strategy"). This protocol prevents IP leakage and model commoditization.
    
- P3. The "P1-Funds-P2" (The "Tax & Reward"): The "Visible Business" (P1) is the "day job" and its execution is the "tax" that must be paid. The "Hidden Business" (P2) is the "night job" and is the "reward." P2 "tinkering" is only unlocked by completing P1 tasks. This prevents P2 "Priority Hijack" (procrasti-building).
    
- P4. The "Fixed-Price" Mandate (The "Anti-Mentor" Model): We only sell fixed-price, outcome-based products (Initium). We never sell time ("day rates"). Accepting linear, time-based work is a fatal error, as it is not structured to feed the Codex (violating P1), and it signals a "vendor" not an "architect" (violating P2).

## 6.0 The 90-Day "Cold Start" Plan (P1 & P2 Parallel Tracks)

### 6.1 Priority 1 (P1): Data Acquisition & First Sale (The "Day Job")

- Days 1-30: Market Intel Campaign (C3 Calls)
    

- Action: Execute the SOP - Strategic Network Engagement (V1.2), focusing on the Primary Target Segments (see GTM Plan).
    
- Goal: Secure 10-15 conversations with C3: Market Intel and C1: Strategic Navigator contacts (e.g., UoW, Monash, Port Kembla contacts).
    
- Output: 3-5 validated, recurring "Messy Problems" (this becomes the Initium pitch) and 2-3 warm introductions to C4: Economic Buyers.
    
- P2 Prerequisite: This activity funds the P2 build time (per P3. P1-Funds-P2) and provides the data for the P2 build (per P1. Data Mandate).
    

- Days 31-60: Pitch Refinement & C4 Targeting
    

- Action: Use the "Messy Problem" intel (now in Codex V0.0) to refine the $15k Initium pitch for the Primary Target Segments.
    
- Goal: Pitch the Initium diagnostic to the C4 contacts.
    
- Output: At least one C4 contact in a formal "sales" conversation (SOW drafting stage).
    

- Days 61-90: Close "Beta Client 01"
    

- Action: Secure sign-off on the first $15k Initium project.
    
- Goal: Move from "zero" to "one."
    
- Output: The first paid, proprietary dataset from a Primary Target Segment enters the Codex via the V0.1 Initium Tool.
    

### 6.2 Priority 2 (P2): "Just-in-Time" Build (The "Night Job")

- Days 1-30: Build the "Codex Vessel" (SOP Assistant)
    

- Action: Build the V0.1 Praxis Forge script (per P3. P1-Funds-P2).
    
- Function: A simple Node.js/Python script that watches your Obsidian vault (or Contact Tracker) and automatically parses your C3 call notes, structuring and ingesting the "Key Language" and "Messy Problems" into your Firestore Codex.
    
- Strategic Purpose: This aligns P2 "tinkering" to directly serve P1's data acquisition (per P1. Data Mandate).
    

- Days 31-60: Build the Initium Tool (Internal-Facing V0.1)
    

- Action: Build the V0.1 of the AI Strategy Navigator as an internal-facing tool (per File 1, but not public).
    
- Function: This is the app you will use during the paid $15k Initium to guide your questions, force consistency, and capture client answers directly into the Codex.
    
- Strategic Purpose: This is the "Trojan Horse" in action. You are building the "Hidden Business" while executing the "Visible Business."
    

- Days 61-90: Build the Colophon (V0.1)
    

- Action: Build the V0.1 Colophon node.
    
- Function: A simple, secure web app (e.g., on Cloud Run) to send to "Beta Client 01" to get their digital sign-off on the Initium diagnostic, capturing their testimonial.
    
- Strategic Purpose: This is the first "commit" to your IP moat and the first live data for your Codex Index.

- Full Praxis Forge (File 4): Deferred. Building it now violates P3 (P1-Funds-P2) as it's not "just-in-time" for the P1 sales cycle.
    
- Broad Market Outreach: Deferred. Chasing non-core segments violates Risk 5 (Data Dilution).
