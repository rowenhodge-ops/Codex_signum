## 1.0 Core GTM Principle: The "Beachhead"

This GTM plan is designed to solve the "Cold Start" problem for the Codex (the "Hidden Business," P1. Data Mandate). The Codex is a "knowledge graph," and a graph is only as valuable as the density and quality of its nodes.

Therefore, our 90-day "Cold Start" plan is not to chase all revenue. It is to dominate a single, data-rich "beachhead" segment.

We will seed the Codex with the proprietary "messy problems" of one vertical. This creates a high-density data cluster, which in turn:

1. Validates the $15k Initium product.
    
2. Creates a repeatable Fabrica (implementation) playbook.
    
3. Fuels the Codex Index (marketing) with peer-validated ROI.
    

The Higher Education (HEI) segment is the clear primary target ("The Whale"). However, your concern about "concentration risk" is valid. We will therefore also target a high-potential alternative segment in parallel ("The Dolphin").
